About Us

Box Subscription Service

If you love trying new loose-leaf teas of premium quality, you have come to the right place. Try one of our subscription boxes and discover some truly great teas from China, Sri Lanka, South Africa and many more.

We have subscriptions to suit everyone and offer monthly, quarterly and semiannual delivery. All boxes contain 6 pouches of tea and some biscuits to enjoy with your tea. You will also get a booklet with recipes using tea.

And we offer FREE worldwide-delivery!

About True Brew

True Brew was founded in 2014 by a couple of friends who actually met and bonded over a cup of tea in a cafe in 2010!

After getting tired of us complaining about the meagre selection of good tea available in the supermarkets, another friend said: "why don't you just open your own tea store?" and here we are!

We love sharing our passion for delicious tea with people all over the world, and we are always happy to hear from customers, so if you have suggestions or just want to say hi, feel free to contact us through the Contact page.
